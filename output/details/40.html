VALIC: One-on-One Retirement Counseling Sessions
VALIC, one of Duke's retirement plan providers, offers faculty and staff one-on-one retirement counseling sessions. Please contact the Investment Representative to schedule a one-on-one session to discuss your investments. Bring any retirement material you have that you would like to discuss in further detail.
