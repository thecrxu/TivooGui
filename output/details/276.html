Shabbat with Jewish Life at Duke - Reform & Conservative Services and Benenson Family Shabbat Dinner
Celebrate Shabbat with student-led Reform and Conservative services and enjoy a delicious FREE dinner made possible by the generosity of the Benenson Family. Services begin at 6:15p and are followed by a family-style dinner at 7:15p every week during the academic year that school is in session. To guarantee your spot at dinner, please RSVP at http://www.studentaffairs.duke.edu/jewishlife/shabbat-dinner-form by 12noon on Friday.
