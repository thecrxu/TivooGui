Platanos y Collard Greens
PLATANOS Y COLLARD GREENS: Don't miss this! Presented by the Department of African & African American Studies, the Program in Latino/a Studies, and the Center for Multicultural Affairs, the hit romantic comedy performance, "Platanos Y Collard Greens," is coming to Duke on September 29th (7pm, Reynolds Theater)! Check out the website www.platanosandcollardgreens.com/ Tickets are free at the door to first 600 attendees. Follow-up panel discussion will take place Tuesday, Oct 4th at 7pm. You don't want to miss this performance and discussion! Additional co-sponsors: Duke Latino/a Graduate Student Association, Program in Latina/o Studies at UNC-Chapel Hill, Spanish Service-Learning, The Carolina Latina/o Collaborative
